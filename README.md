# 🧠 NeuroAdapt – AI Cognitive Productivity Dashboard

> An intelligent system that analyzes user behavior patterns to detect focus levels, cognitive stress, and productivity trends — and provides real-time actionable insights.

---

## 🚀 Why This Project?

In today's world, most people:

* Work long hours but feel unproductive
* Get distracted without realizing it
* Experience burnout without early signals

**NeuroAdapt aims to solve this silently.**

Instead of asking users how they feel, it observes **behavior patterns** and intelligently responds.

---

## 💡 What It Does

NeuroAdapt acts like a **personal productivity intelligence system**:

* 📊 Tracks behavioral signals (typing, app usage, session time)
* 🧠 Estimates **focus level & cognitive stress**
* ⚡ Provides **real-time recommendations**
* 📈 Visualizes productivity trends

---

## 🧪 Real-World Use Cases (Simple but Powerful)

### 🧑‍🎓 Smart Study Companion

Detects low focus during study sessions and suggests:

> “Take a break or switch topic”

---

### 👨‍💻 Developer Productivity Tracker

Identifies deep work patterns:

> “You were most productive between 9–11 PM”

---

### ⚠️ Early Burnout Detection

Monitors subtle signals like:

* frequent tab switching
* typing slowdowns

and alerts before burnout happens.

---

### 📊 Habit Intelligence

Learns your behavior over time and helps optimize:

> “Your best focus window is in the morning”
